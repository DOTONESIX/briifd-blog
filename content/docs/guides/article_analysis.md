One of the most powerful features of having an AI media analyst is the ability to search and filter across thousands of articles in seconds. This guide will show you how to use the AI assistant to analyze articles and extract insights.

## Analyzing Articles
Bruno has access to all articles for the Search Terms you have set up. You can ask Bruno to search for articles based on keywords, locations, sentiment, impressions, and more. Here are some examples of how you can analyze articles using the AI assistant:
- **Title Keywords**: Search for articles with specific keywords in the title.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Filter articles based on positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.
- **Location**: Search for articles based on the country the content is addressing.
- **Impressions**: Filter articles based on the number of impressions.
- **Date Range**: Search for articles published within a specific date range.

## Extracting Insights
Once you have filtered the articles, you can ask Bruno to extract insights from the content. Bruno can summarize the key points, detect the overall sentiment, and identify the main topics discussed in the articles. Here are some examples of how you can extract insights using the AI assistant:
- **Summarize**: Ask Bruno to summarize the key points of the articles.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Ask Bruno to detect the overall sentiment of the articles.
- **Themes**: Ask Bruno to identify the main themes discussed in the articles.

## Conclusion
Analyzing articles using the AI assistant is a powerful way to extract insights quickly and efficiently. By leveraging the AI assistant, you can save time and resources while gaining valuable insights from the content you are monitoring. Try using the AI assistant to analyze articles and see how it can help you make better decisions based on the data you collect.
